Nigredo

Controlled sonic degradation. Nigredo operates on the principle of reduction before reconstruction — raw signal is passed through structured distortion stages, stripped of inessential content, and reshaped into dense, deliberate texture. The process is subtractive by nature. What remains is form derived from noise.

Parameters

  • Nigredo Parallel mix control — dry/wet balance of the degraded signal
  • Drive Distortion intensity — continuous blending through saturation stages
  • LP Focus Low-pass filter before fuzz — 0 to 200 Hz isolation of sub content
  • Post Tone High-pass shaping after distortion — removes low-end residue up to 350 Hz
